The Fault in Our Stars


The Fault in Our Stars depicts the sad story of two teenagers with terminal illnesses, but its ending shares a moving message about life, death, and finding happiness. Adapted from the bestselling young adult novel by John Green, the 2014 drama sees Hazel Grace Lancaster (Shailene Woodley) and Gus Waters (Ansel Elgort) meet at a support group.


Coming of Age | Romance | Tragedy


Director Josh Boone
Writers Michael H. Weber  John Green
Stars Shailene Woodley  Ansel Elgort  Nat Wolff

DOTS


“Descendants of the Sun” (DOTS), centers around a special forces captain, Yoo Shi Jin (Song Joong Ki), and a doctor, Kang Mo Yeon (Song Hye Kyo), who form a romantic connection when they meet in Korea, but sadly part ways when the doctor struggles with the conflict between the Hippocratic Oath she swore as a doctor and the nature of the duty of a special forces soldier.


Action | Comedy | Romance | Drama


Director Baek Sang-Hoon  Lee Eung-bok
Writers Kim Eun-sook MWon-Suk Kim
Stars Song Joong-ki Song Hye-kyo  Jin Goo

Game of Thrones


At its heart, Game of Thrones tells the story of several noble families vying for control of the Iron Throne—the symbol of power in the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros.The Starks are the noble northerners, the Lannisters are the wealthy schemers, and the Targaryens? Well, they bring dragons to the party.


Action Epic | Adventure Epic | Dark Fantasy


Director David Nutter  Alan Taylor
Writers David Benitoff Dave Hill  D.B. Weiss
Stars Peter Dinklage  Lena Headey  Kit Harrington  Emilia Clarke

VIKINGS


The adventures of Ragnar Lothbrok, the greatest hero of his age. The series tells the sagas of Ragnar's band of Viking brothers and his family, as he rises to become King of the Viking tribes. As well as being a fearless warrior, Ragnar embodies the Norse traditions of devotion to the gods.


Historical Epic | War Epic | Action


Director Ciaran Donnelly ken Girotti
Writers Micheal Hirst
Stars Katheryn Winnick  Travis Fimmel

Unravels the mystery of a nightmarish town in middle America that traps all those who enter. Alice in Borderland


"Alice in Borderland" follows Ryohei Arisu, a listless and video game-obsessed young man, who finds himself in a deserted, parallel Tokyo along with his friends. They are forced to participate in dangerous games where the stakes are life and death. Each game is represented by playing cards, with the difficulty and type of game indicated by the card's suit and number.


Cyber Thriller | Sci-Fi | Survival


Director Shinsuke sato
Writers Haro Aso   Shinsuke Sato
Stars kento Yamazaki  Tao Tsuchiya
